Installation & Care Instructions

Installation Guidelines

Proper installation is crucial to ensure the longevity and aesthetic appeal of your Classic Impressions pieces. We recommend the following steps for a successful installation: • Surface Preparation: Ensure that the surface is clean, dry, and free from dust or grease. • Adhesive Selection: Use a high-quality construction adhesive suitable for the material of your piece and the substrate. • Positioning: Dry-fit the piece to determine the best placement before applying adhesive. • Application: Apply adhesive evenly to the back of the piece and press firmly into place. Use temporary supports if necessary until the adhesive cures. • Curing Time: Allow adequate curing time as per the adhesive manufacturer's instructions before handling or painting.

Care and Maintenance

To maintain the beauty and integrity of your Classic Impressions pieces, follow these care guidelines: • Regular Dusting: Use a soft, dry cloth or a feather duster to remove dust regularly. • Cleaning: For more thorough cleaning, use a damp cloth with mild soap and water. Avoid abrasive cleaners or solvents. • Avoid Moisture: While our materials are durable, prolonged exposure to moisture can affect finishes. Wipe off any spills immediately. • Touch-Ups: For minor scratches or chips, contact our customer service for advice on touch-up kits or professional refinishing services.

Professional Assistance

For complex installations or restorations, we recommend consulting with a professional installer or contractor experienced in working with decorative architectural elements.
