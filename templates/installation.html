Installation And Care Instructions

Preserving Your Investment

The pieces in the Classic Impressions collection are designed for seamless integration into sophisticated architectural environments. We have engineered our products to facilitate installation by skilled tradesmen and to require minimal, simple maintenance.

Care and Maintenance

Our durable finishes are sealed for protection and designed to be virtually maintenance-free.

  • Cleaning: To clean your Classic Impressions piece, simply use a soft, dry, lint-free cloth or a feather duster.
  • Stubborn Marks: If necessary, a slightly dampened cloth (water only) may be used very gently. Avoid all abrasive cleaners, harsh chemicals (like ammonia or bleach), and solvents, as these can damage the protective sealant and the integrity of the hand-applied patina.
  • Environment: Our products are designed for interior, and exterior usage.

By following these simple guidelines, your architectural elements will retain their unique character and sophisticated Classic Impression for decades.

Our lightweight yet durable composite structure simplifies handling compared to solid stone or metal.

  • Integrated Mounting: Structural pieces (Corbels, Niches, Pediments) are supplied with integrated mounting points (e.g., keyholes or pre-drilled brackets) designed for secure attachment to studs or structural blocking.
  • Adhesion for Reliefs: Decorative reliefs and appliqu√©s (Cartouches, Emblems) can be securely attached using a high-quality structural adhesive (such as construction-grade epoxy or polyurethane adhesive) appropriate for both the product and the substrate material (e.g., drywall, wood, masonry).
  • Professional Recommendation: We strongly recommend that all complex or permanent architectural pieces be installed by a qualified professional (e.g., licensed contractor, carpenter, or millworker) to ensure safe, secure, and perfectly level placement.
  • Finishing at Seams: For built-in elements like niches or large corbels, minor seams between the product and the wall can be professionally finished using a non-shrinking, paintable filler or caulk before the final wall paint is applied.
