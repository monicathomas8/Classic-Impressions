Installation And Care Instructions

The Classic Impressions collection is crafted for lasting beauty with minimal upkeep. Each piece is engineered for straightforward installation by skilled tradespeople. Our durable finishes are sealed for protection and designed to be virtually maintenance-free. To ensure your architectural elements remain stunning for years to come, please follow these simple care and installation guidelines:

Cleaning
To clean your Classic Impressions piece, simply use a soft, dry, lint-free cloth or a feather duster.

Stubborn Marks
If necessary, a slightly dampened cloth (water only) may be used very gently. Avoid all abrasive cleaners, harsh chemicals (like ammonia or bleach), and solvents, as these can damage the protective sealant and the integrity of the hand-applied patina.

Environment
Our products are designed for interior, and exterior usage.

Integrated Mounting
Structural pieces (Corbels, Niches, Pediments) are supplied with integrated mounting points (e.g., keyholes or pre-drilled brackets) designed for secure attachment to studs or structural blocking.

Adhesion for Reliefs
Decorative reliefs and appliqués (Cartouches, Emblems) can be securely attached using a high-quality structural adhesive (such as construction-grade epoxy or polyurethane adhesive) appropriate for both the product and the substrate material (e.g., drywall, wood, masonry).

Professional Recommendation
We strongly recommend that all complex or permanent architectural pieces be installed by a qualified professional (e.g., licensed contractor, carpenter, or millworker) to ensure safe, secure, and perfectly level placement.

Finishing at Seams
For built-in elements like niches or large corbels, minor seams between the product and the wall can be professionally finished using a non-shrinking, paintable filler or caulk before the final wall paint is applied.
