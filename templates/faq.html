Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive about our products and services. If you have a question that isn't answered here, please feel free to contact us.

What is Classic Impressions?

Classic Impressions specializes in high-quality architectural details and decorative pieces that bring classical elegance to interior spaces. Our products are crafted using advanced materials and traditional techniques to ensure durability and exquisite detail.

What materials are used in your products?

We use a proprietary High-Density Architectural Composite resin known for its exceptional detail retention, durability, and stability.

How do I care for and maintain my Classic Impressions pieces?

Our pieces are designed for easy maintenance. Regular dusting and occasional cleaning with a damp cloth will keep them looking their best. Avoid harsh chemicals.

Do you offer custom finishes?

Yes, we offer bespoke finishes including Aged Limestone, Verdigris Copper, and Faux Marble to suit your design needs.

Can I order a sample before making a purchase?

Please contact our customer service team to inquire about sample availability for specific products.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the items are in their original condition. Please refer to our return policy page for more details.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary based on location and product availability. Typically, orders are processed within 3-5 business days and shipping can take an additional 5-10 business days.
